## 💡 Judge Q&A - Quick Answers

**Q: What makes this AI-driven?**
A: 6 AI engines for prediction, optimization, decision-making. Remove AI = useless system.

**Q: Why not LLM for everything?**
A: Right tool for each job. Time-series for forecasting, optimization for constraints, LLM for explanations only.

**Q: How do you handle cost?**
A: Batch processing, caching, no LLM for predictions. ₹50/shop/month.

**Q: Is this production-ready?**

**Q: What if AI is removed?**

**Q: How is this different?**
